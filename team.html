Team

Experienced leadership. Nigerian roots.

Gaxon's team brings together senior expertise in energy engineering, project finance, and infrastructure development — with a track record built on delivering real megawatt capacity in Nigeria.

Chief Executive Officer

Dafini Nelson Jaja

Dafini is a seasoned management consultant and finance professional with 11 years of experience spanning investment banking, project finance, venture capital, and financial advisory services across multiple industries. He has advised on several renewable energy, solar commercial and industrial, and power generation projects, and leads Gaxon's overall strategy, business development, and stakeholder relationships.

Chief Operating Officer

Oludeji Owoade

Oludeji brings over 20 years of hands-on experience across the Nigerian energy sector, with expertise spanning power generation, transmission, distribution, metering, operations and maintenance, and oil and gas. He previously served as a key contributor at Negris Limited, where he was instrumental in developing approximately 100 MW of power generation capacity in Nigeria — a track record that directly informs how Gaxon designs, constructs, and operates its plants.

Infrastructure Finance Partners

Bankable transaction support

Gaxon's infrastructure finance partners brings over 14 years of experience in project bidding, acquisitions, construction, operations, monetisation, and exits across the African infrastructure sector. A former Director at DC Africa — an EPC business and Asset Management Company with equity investments in infrastructure projects — the team's financial structuring capabilities ensure that every Gaxon project is built on a bankable, investor-ready foundation.
