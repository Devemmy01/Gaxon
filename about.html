Our Story

A decade of energy infrastructure development.

Gaxon Energy is a Nigerian independent power producer and energy infrastructure developer, incorporated in 2014. We specialise in the development, financing, construction, and operation of off-grid and captive power plants serving government and commercial clients across Nigeria.

Our mission

To close Africa’s Infrastructure gap by developing reliable, technology-proven independent power plants — delivered on time, financed efficiently, and operated to global standards.

Our Values

A standard of excellence

Africa's energy transition demands more than projects. It demands a standard that we uphold in every megawatt we deliver.

Excellence in execution

We apply international engineering rigour and project management discipline to every phase, from feasibility to commercial operation.

Integrity in every transaction

We build trust through transparent structuring, honest feasibility assessments, and commitments we keep.

Technology that works

Every asset we deploy is selected for field-tested reliability and suitability for the African operating environment.

Resilience by design

We build redundancy into every project, ensuring plants keep running even when conditions are difficult.

Impact at scale

Powering progress

We are not building small. Gaxon's ambition is to contribute meaningfully to Africa's energy capacity — developing projects that power government institutions and anchor industrial activity.
