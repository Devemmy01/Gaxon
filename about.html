About Gaxon

A decade of energy infrastructure development in Nigeria.

Gaxon Energy is a Nigerian independent power producer and energy infrastructure developer, incorporated in 2014. We specialise in the development, financing, construction, and operation of off-grid and captive power plants serving government and commercial clients across Nigeria.

Our mission

Closing Africa's infrastructure gap.

We develop reliable, technology-proven independent power plants — delivered on time, financed efficiently, and operated to global standards.

  • Model: Build-Own-Operate under Public-Private Partnership frameworks.
  • Focus: Government, commercial, and industrial offtakers.
  • Standard: Bankable delivery and disciplined operations.
Our values

We measure ourselves against the best in the world.

Africa's energy transition demands more than projects. It demands a standard.

Excellence in execution.

We apply the same engineering rigour and project management discipline that governs power development in the world's most demanding markets. From feasibility through to commercial operation, every phase is held to international benchmarks — because the cost of underperformance in power infrastructure is measured in lives, livelihoods, and lost productivity.

Integrity in every transaction.

The energy sector in Africa has a long history of projects that were announced and never delivered. We hold ourselves to a different standard — one built on transparent structuring, honest feasibility, and commitments we keep. Our partners and offtakers know exactly what they are getting before they sign.

Technology that works.

We are technology-agnostic in principle but uncompromising in quality. Whether gas or solar, every generation asset we deploy is selected for proven performance, field-tested reliability, and suitability for the African operating environment — not lowest cost at the point of procurement.

Resilience by design.

Africa's energy infrastructure cannot afford fragility. We build redundancy into every project — backup systems, fuel supply contingencies, and O&M protocols that keep plants running when conditions are difficult. Reliability is not a feature. It is the product.

Impact at scale.

We are not building small. Gaxon's ambition is to contribute meaningfully to Africa's energy capacity — developing projects that power government institutions, anchor industrial activity, and create long-term employment. Every megawatt we commission is a step toward a continent that no longer tolerates energy poverty as an inevitability.

Partnership over transaction.

We treat every engagement as a long-term operating relationship, not a one-time contract. That mindset shapes how we negotiate, build, communicate, and deliver.
