Services

End-to-end independent power development.

Gaxon manages the full project lifecycle — from initial energy audit and feasibility through to construction, commissioning, and long-term operations. Our clients receive a single accountable partner across every phase.

⚡

Gas-fired IPP development

We develop modular gas-fired power plants from 1 MW to 50 MW using compressed natural gas (CNG) or piped natural gas supply. Plants are designed for scalability — modules can be added as demand grows — and include full balance-of-plant, dedicated distribution networks, and diesel backup systems.

☀

Utility-scale solar power plant development

We develop large-scale solar photovoltaic power plants for government institutions, state utilities, and industrial clients across Nigeria and West Africa. Our solar projects are standalone utility-scale facilities — purpose-built power plants designed to deliver consistent, clean electricity at the scale that governments and large commercial offtakers actually require.

Projects are structured as long-term captive supply or grid-connected agreements, giving offtakers a fixed, predictable energy cost over the PPA term and giving investors a bankable, asset-backed revenue stream.

🛠

Operations & Maintenance

Post-commissioning, Gaxon provides full operations and preventive maintenance services for all plants we develop.

📈

Project finance & PPP structuring

For government clients and development partners seeking advisory support, our team draws on deep experience in investment banking and infrastructure finance to structure PPP transactions, prepare bankable project documentation, and navigate the approval and financial close process.
